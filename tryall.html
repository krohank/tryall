Wildlife Wonders

Introduction to Wildlife

Wildlife refers to undomesticated animal species, but has come to include all plants, fungi, and other organisms that grow or live wild in an area without being introduced by humans.

Importance of Wildlife

Wildlife is crucial for maintaining ecological balance. Each species in an ecosystem plays a role in the food chain and contributes to the health of the environment.

Common Wildlife Animals

  • Lion
  • Elephant
  • Tiger
  • Kangaroo
  • Giraffe

Conservation Efforts

Efforts to conserve wildlife are critical as many species face threats from habitat loss, climate change, and poaching. Conservation programs aim to protect species and restore habitats to ensure biodiversity for future generations.
